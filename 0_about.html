UX & UI Designer

Sandy Chang

Art and Design has always been my biggest passion in life. After my college education, I began working in graphic design and web development for companies in the United States and Taiwan, encompassing small businesses, nonprofits, e-commerce, and enterprise industry. Design projects I have been involved in include but not limited to websites, digital/printed marketing materials, and web/mobile applications. My passion for usability research and interface design grew as I designed multiple enterprise applications where I was the lead designer for projects in web and mobile. I hope to design user-centered products that are not only looking good, but also making an impact to our everyday lives, putting a smile on all of our faces.

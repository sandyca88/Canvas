Project Info:

InTouch will be the "Yelp for Cisco IT". Service Owners need a way to aggregate and quantify the sentiment of their services and assets, while not inundating end users with ad hoc surveys. InTouch looks to provide added capabilities allowing Service Owners to take action, based upon feedback. One challenge to the realizing this vision is in the ability to encourage employees to leave feedback.
